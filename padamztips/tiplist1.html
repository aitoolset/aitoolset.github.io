Loadout Info

  1. 1. Bags: Remember to return all bags. They are mandatory for use and must be returned. 1. Bolsas: Recuerden devolver todas las bolsas. Son de uso obligatorio y deben retornarse.
  2. 2. Rain : Drive with caution. Reduce your speed and increase your braking distance. 2. Lluvia: Conduzcan con prevaucion. Reduzcan la velocicdad y aumenten la distancia de frenado.
  3. 3. Engine : Turn off the vehicle at each stop. It is safer and more efficient 3. Motor: Apaguen el vehiculo en cada parada (stop). Es mas seguro y eficiente.
  4. 4. Anti-Theft Security: Always secure your vehicle. If the remote doesn't lock it, notify the closer immediately to get it fixed. 4. Seguridad Antirrobo: Aseguren siempre su vehiculo. Si el control no lo bloquea, avisen de inmediato al closer para solucionarlo.
  5. 5. Warehouse : The use of headphones/earbuds inside the warehouse is prohibited. 5. Bodega : Prohibido el uso de auriculares en la bodega.
